Our Services

Dedicated craftsmanship for every timepiece, new or old.

Expert Repair & Servicing

A fine watch is a precision instrument. We provide comprehensive servicing to ensure it runs flawlessly. Our services include movement overhaul, water resistance testing, case and bracelet refinishing, and battery replacement for quartz models. We treat every watch with the respect and care it deserves.

Request a Repair Quote

Vintage Restoration

Heirlooms carry stories. Our passion is to preserve them. We specialize in the delicate art of vintage watch restoration, carefully sourcing period-correct parts or fabricating them when necessary. Our goal is to bring your cherished timepiece back to life while honoring its history and character.

Discuss a Restoration

Full Custom Builds

For those who want a watch that is truly their own. We guide you through a creative process to design and build a unique timepiece from the ground up. Select every component, from the case and movement to the dial and hands, to create a watch that tells your personal story.
