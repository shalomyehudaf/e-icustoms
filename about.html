Where Time Meets Your Story

It all started with a friend's challenge...

That simple question—"Why don't you build your own watch?"—sparked an unexpected journey. From crafting my very first custom timepiece at a small workbench, I discovered a deep passion for design, precision, and the art of storytelling through watches. Today, Ethan & Isaac Customs exists to reinvent the custom watch experience.

More Than Customization. Creation.

We're not just changing straps or engraving initials—we're building watches from the ground up, piece by piece, inspired by you. Whether you know exactly what you want or are just starting to explore ideas, we'll guide you through every step. From selecting components to fine-tuning the smallest details, we work together until your watch feels like a true extension of who you are.

This isn't about fleeting trends. It's about creating a personal, lasting statement.

Let's build something that's truly yours.
